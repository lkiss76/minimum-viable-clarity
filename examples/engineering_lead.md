## Intent
Create a risk-focused summary of an architectural proposal.

## Context
The CTO needs a concise view of risks before approving the proposal.

## Criteria
- Max 6 bullet points  
- Must include: risk source, impact, likelihood, mitigation  
- No marketing or visionary language  

## Exemplar
- **Risk:** Dependency on unstable third-party API  
- **Impact:** Potential authentication outages  
- **Likelihood:** Medium  
- **Mitigation:** Implement fallback and caching layer
